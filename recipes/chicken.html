Easy Homemade Food with Chicken and Rice:

This is an easy-to-cook recipe of homemade cat food that you cats will love!

EQUIPMENT:

  • Large bowl
  • Spoon
  • Airtight container (for storage)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 3 oz baked dark chicken meat
  • 1/4 cup boiled white rice
  • 1/4 cup boiled potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon Rx Vitamins Nutritional Support

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Mix all these ingredients together and the meal is ready.
  2. Store the leftovers in you Airtight container in the refrigerator for future use.
